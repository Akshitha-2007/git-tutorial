ABOUT FRUITS

STRAWBERRIES

Strawberries are popular, red, heart-shaped fruits from the rose family that are rich in vitamins, especially vitamin C. Although not true berries, they are enjoyed fresh or used in many prepared foods and are cultivated worldwide.



MANGOES

Mango is a tropical fruit, known as the "king of fruits," native to South Asia and belonging to the cashew family. It is a popular fruit cultivated for over 4,000 years, with India producing the widest variety.

BLUEBERRIES

Blueberries are a nutritious fruit native to North America, known for being rich in antioxidants, vitamins (\(C\) and \(K\)), and fiber, and are linked to numerous health benefits.
